## Make Memories Last a Lifetime

Whether you are seeking a romantic getaway or adventure for you and your friend or lover, this unique body painting experience for two will be lots of fun and will capture memories to cherish for years to come. This experience could be an unforgettable gift as a pre wedding session, anniversary, birthday or any other occasion.

## Body Painting Session

A body painting session normally lasts for 1-2 hours per person. An afternoon session consists of:

- You can choose the painting from the list of 12 popular paintings below or your own design (with same level of complexity and coverage)
- Private body painting session
- Free consultation

## Photography Service

After the painting is done, the newly painted masterpiece on your body will be captured with Professional Studio Photography Session.

Photography service includes:

- 1 hour studio photography (your friend/partner is welcome to join the shoot)
- Private online gallery for images from your session
- $150 credit for any print order

## Frequently Asked Questions

**I've never done a photo shoot before. Will I get help with posing?**
Yes, our photographer will provide some basic directions and posing suggestions to help you look your best.

**Can my friend come to the session?**
You are more than welcome to bring a friend to the session as long as that person does not interfere with the creative process.

**Will I get individual shots?**
Yes, each person will have individual shoots then we'll do combined shots together.

**Can you retouch my photo and add creative background/treatment?**
Yes, we also offer retouching services for additional cost. The cost for creative background/treatment depends on the complexity of the project.

**I have a specific painting that I want but it's not listed here, can you do it?**
If you have a particular painting that you want to do, please let us know during your consultation.
